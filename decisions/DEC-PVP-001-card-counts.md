# DEC-PVP-001 — Card counts for v0.1 (6/18/6/6)

**Date**: 2026-06-20
**Status**: v0.1 (revisit after first playtest)

## Decision

v0.1 ships 36 cards split as:
- 6 Meetings
- 18 Proposals
- 6 Policies
- 6 Exposures

## Why these numbers

- **6 Meetings = 6 rounds**. Each round flips one Meeting; the deck cycles exactly through the game. No deck reshuffle mid-game keeps cognitive load low for a first playtest.

- **18 Proposals = ~3 per player at 5–6 players, with refill room**. A 5-card hand of Proposals + 2 Exposures is a typical opening; 18 Proposals supports refill across 6 rounds without forcing a reshuffle in a 5-player game.

- **6 Policies as a shared pool, not in hands**. Players grab from the table mid-round under specific card effects. 6 is small enough to read at a glance.

- **6 Exposures = 2 per opening hand for a 3-player game, scaling down with player count**. Exposures are powerful disruption; keeping the count tight makes each use feel weighty rather than spammy.

## What revisits this DEC

After the first playtest, capture:
- Did anyone run out of Proposals before round 6?
- Were Exposures spammed (too many in hand) or dormant (too few)?
- Did the 6-round arc feel like one too few, one too many, or correct?
- Did Policies sit unused in the pool, or were they regularly grabbed?

DEC-PVP-002+ adjusts counts if the playtest surfaces a real imbalance.

## Out of scope

- Multiple decks (PMs vs Engineers vs Sales). Each is a v1.0+ pack.
- Card-rarity tiers. v0.1 is flat.
- Localization. English-only for v0.1.
