M-01 meeting
All-Hands
Applause tokens count double this round. Boo tokens convert to red flags.
— Stand up. Greet the table as if they are 9,000 employees on a Zoom call.
The morale survey is open until Friday.
M-02 meeting
Sprint Review
Only actions you can mime score full reputation. Verbal-only proposals score half.
— Mime the work item before you read its effect.
M-03 meeting
Board Update
Speak only in metrics or metaphors this round. Table votes on which language did better.
— Replace every adjective on your card with the word "north-star" before reading.
M-04 meeting
Reorg Roulette
Shuffle all in-play Policies. Re-deal one to each player. Everyone must justify why this was inevitable.
— Begin your justification with "as we have been saying for some time".
M-05 meeting
Quarterly Roadmap Lock
No Exposures may be played this round. All Proposals must be framed as commitments.
— Sign each Proposal with an imaginary pen and pass it to the player on your left.
M-06 meeting
Skip-Level
The player with the lowest reputation chooses one card from each opponent's hand to set aside.
— Address the highest-reputation player as if they have asked you about your career goals.
They almost certainly did not ask.
P-01 proposal
Quarterly Moonshot
Promise 10x. If table laughs, +3 reputation, +1 red flag. If table is silent, +1 reputation only.
— Use the phrase "non-linear growth" at least twice.
P-02 proposal
Cross-Functional Offsite
Everyone switches seats. You draw 3 Proposals. If anyone refuses to move, you gain +2 reputation for "driving change."
— Read this card while standing in a different player's chair.
P-03 proposal
Hire-To-Plan Defense
Justify a hire you didn't need. Table votes; if more than half nod sagely, +2 reputation.
— Use the phrase "we are leaning in here" and pause.
P-04 proposal
Vendor Re-Eval
Pick another player's Policy. Reframe it as your initiative. If they don't push back, take +1 reputation and the Policy.
— Read the Policy aloud as if you wrote it.
They will push back.
P-05 proposal
Pre-Read Distribution
Slide one of your other Proposals under the table. It resolves at end of round with no votes.
— Mutter the contents of the pre-read as a sidebar to no one.
P-06 proposal
Strategic Pause
Skip your turn. Gain +1 reputation. Lose -1 if the round still ends in your favor.
— Cross your arms and look thoughtful for five seconds.
P-07 proposal
Roadmap Tightening
Discard a Proposal from your hand. Gain +2 reputation. The discarded Proposal becomes a Red Flag for the next round.
— Announce the cut as if it was your idea all along.
P-08 proposal
Customer Anecdote
Tell a story about a customer the table cannot verify. If no one asks for sources, +2 reputation.
— Begin with "I was just on the phone with one of our largest customers."
P-09 proposal
Brown-Bag Talk
Volunteer to teach the team something. Gain +1 reputation. Lose -1 if the topic is your own past mistake.
— Say "this is a learning organization" at the end.
P-10 proposal
Calendar Defense
Decline a meeting in front of the table. Gain +1 reputation. Lose -1 if the meeting was yours.
— Send the decline aloud, in real time, with a one-sentence rationale.
P-11 proposal
New Framework Adoption
Pick a noun from another player's card. Append "framework" to it. Propose adopting it across the org. +2 reputation if table cannot recall what it does in 10 seconds.
— Draw the framework as a 2x2 in the air.
P-12 proposal
Listening Tour
Ask each player one open-ended question. Take their answers as inputs to a future Proposal. +1 reputation per answer you do not interrupt.
— Make notes on an imaginary pad after each answer.
The notes do not need to relate to the answers.
P-13 proposal
Stretch Goal
Add a number to the current Meeting card. The table must vote whether the number is "ambitious" or "unrealistic." +2 reputation on either verdict.
— Underline the number on the table with your finger.
P-14 proposal
Cross-Org Alignment
Name two Proposals on the table. Declare them "aligned." Both owners gain +1 reputation; you gain +2.
— Use the word "synergies" exactly once and apologize for it.
P-15 proposal
Bias For Action
Resolve the current Meeting card immediately without further discussion. Gain +1 reputation. Lose -2 if anyone protests.
— Stand. Pick up the Meeting card. Place it face down.
P-16 proposal
Mentorship Hour
Offer feedback to the player on your right. They may accept or refuse. On accept, +1 reputation to both. On refuse, +1 red flag to you.
— Begin the feedback with "in the spirit of growth."
P-17 proposal
Vendor Lunch
Trade one of your Proposals for one in another player's hand. Both players gain +1 reputation. The vendor pays.
— Refer to the vendor as "a great partner of ours."
P-18 proposal
Quiet Win
Discard this card without playing it. Gain +1 reputation at end of round.
— Place this card face down in front of you. Say nothing.
The win is in the not-saying.
L-01 policy
OKR Realignment
Numbers do not matter this round; adjectives do. Replace each numeric value on played cards with the nearest adjective.
— Translate "10x" as "transformational" the first time it is read.
L-02 policy
Two-Pizza Team
Hand size = number of pizza slices on the table (real or imagined). If no pizza, hand size = 2.
— Order the imaginary pizza aloud.
L-03 policy
Open-Door Policy
Anyone may interrupt any Proposal once. The interrupter gains +1 reputation if the table acknowledges the interruption.
— Knock on the table before interrupting.
L-04 policy
Data-Driven Mandate
Every Proposal must cite a number. Citations are not verified. Proposals without a number gain no reputation.
— Read every number twice for emphasis.
L-05 policy
Customer Obsession
Each round, the player who mentions "the customer" earliest gets +1 reputation. Subsequent mentions are worth -1.
— Mime cradling a fragile object whenever you say "customer."
L-06 policy
Hiring Freeze
Proposals that add headcount cannot be played. Proposals that "rebalance" the org are unaffected.
— Use the phrase "freeing up capacity" when reading any rebalance.
E-01 exposure
Post-Mortem, Pre-Launch
Point at a played card and ask "what problem does this solve?" If they take more than 10 seconds to answer, they gain 1 red flag; you gain +1 reputation.
— Stand and ask with genuine concern.
E-02 exposure
Continuity Error
The last two cards played by any player cannot be used this round unless someone narrates the missing scene.
— Read this card while looking past the table, as if into a different shot.
E-03 exposure
Audit Trail
Pick a target's reputation gain from the last round. Demand evidence. If they cannot reconstruct the math, reverse the gain.
— Hold an imaginary clipboard.
E-04 exposure
Blameless Post-Mortem
Re-tell another player's failure as a "learning." That player gains -1 red flag. You gain +1 reputation.
— End with "and that's how we got better as a team."
They did not get better as a team.
E-05 exposure
Calendar Subpoena
Demand the target name every meeting they took this round. If they miss one, 1 red flag.
— Open a fictitious calendar app and scroll for effect.
E-06 exposure
Visibility Question
Ask the target "who sees this work?" If they cannot name three stakeholders, their next Proposal cannot score.
— Lean forward and tilt your head slightly.
